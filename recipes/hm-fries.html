Home Made Fries

Description

There's no doubt that french fries are among the most popular sides.

Most people just get the bag of fries from the frozen section and call it a day.

We're staying away from the frozen section here. These home made fries are going to taste nice and fresh.

Here's what you'll need:

  • Potatoes
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Seasonings of Choice

How to:

  1. Pre heat your vegetable oil.
  2. Peel and cut your potatoes into french fry shape.
  3. Fry your cut up potatoes until golden.
  4. Season your fries
