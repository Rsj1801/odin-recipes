Hooot dog

Description

The above image shows off a delcious looking ball park hot dog with a twist.

It's a quick and easy recipe that adds some spice to your everyday hot dog.

Are you ready for the heat? Well, here's what you'll need:

Ingredients:

  • Hot Dogs
  • Buns
  • Nacho Cheese Sauce
  • Flaming Hot Chips(Your choice)

How to:

  1. Boil your hot dogs until fully cooked(NYC style)
  2. Put the hot dogs in the buns.
  3. Next comes the nacho cheese sauce.
  4. Lastly, crush up your flaming hot chips and pour them on top.

That's a hot looking dog!
